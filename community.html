CV & Career Support

Land the Job Before You Land in the Country

Your CV from home won't cut it in Ireland or New Zealand. Employers in both countries have specific expectations — and getting it wrong means your application goes straight to the bin.

Our CV localisation workshops and 1-on-1 coaching sessions (available as an upsell) help you present yourself the way local employers expect.

  • 📄 CV format: Irish and NZ CVs are typically 2 pages, achievement-focused, no photo
  • 🎯 Keywords: ATS optimisation for Irish and NZ job boards
  • 💼 Cover letters: What to include (and what to leave out) for each market
  • 🤝 Interview style: Both markets favour competency-based interviews — we'll prepare you
  • 💰 Salary negotiation: Know your worth and how to ask for it
