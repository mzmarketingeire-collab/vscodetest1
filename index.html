Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

Settle In is a premium platform for skilled professionals planning to relocate to Ireland or New Zealand. Whether you're a construction worker, engineer, nurse, or tech professional — if you're considering making the move, Settle In gives you the guides, community, and expert access to do it with confidence.
We do not provide licensed immigration or legal advice. Our guides are informational resources based on publicly available information and lived experience. We partner with accredited immigration advisors in both Ireland and New Zealand who can assist with your specific visa application — you'll find their details in the Community section.
Settle In Global is our affordable entry-level tier designed for skilled migrants from developing countries who are in the planning phase. Settle In Ireland and Settle In NZ are premium local tiers with deeper country-specific content, live recruiter Q&As, CV workshops, and 1-on-1 coaching — ideal for those making the final move or already in-country.
Our founders have backgrounds in construction recruitment (NZ) and technology recruitment (Ireland), so those sectors are covered in particular depth. However, our guides cover a wide range of skilled professions including engineering, healthcare, IT, finance, and trades. The community includes professionals from many industries who share sector-specific insights.
