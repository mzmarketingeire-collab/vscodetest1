🍀 Ireland — Sample Insight

Construction Sector Update — April 2026

Ireland's construction sector added 2,400 new roles in Q1 2026, driven by the National Development Plan's housing and infrastructure targets. Project managers and civil engineers remain the most in-demand roles, with average salaries up 6% year-on-year.

Regional spotlight: Cork is seeing the highest growth outside Dublin, with 340 new construction roles posted in March alone. The Cork Docklands regeneration project is the key driver.

🥝 New Zealand — Sample Insight

AEWV Policy Update — April 2026

Immigration New Zealand updated the median wage threshold for AEWV applications in March 2026. The new minimum is NZD $29.66/hour (up from $29.00). Employers with existing accreditation are not affected until renewal.

Regional spotlight: Christchurch continues to outperform other regions for construction hiring. The city's rebuild programme has created 1,800 new roles in the past 6 months, with particular demand for quantity surveyors and site managers.
